Our Story

Bhatkya Vimukt Jati Shikshan Sanstha (BVJSS) is a dedicated non-profit organization that has been working tirelessly since 1987 to uplift the lives of marginalized children, especially orphans and children with disabilities. Founded with a vision to provide equal opportunities and holistic development, BVJSS focuses on the care, education, and rehabilitation of orphans, as well as blind, deaf, and differently-abled (Divyang) children.

Situated in a serene and nurturing environment, BVJSS operates a residential facility (hermitage) where around 350 children currently reside. These children are provided with not just basic necessities like food and shelter, but also access to quality education, healthcare, and vocational training. The organization believes in the transformative power of education and strives to empower its students to lead independent and dignified lives.

BVJSS fosters an inclusive atmosphere where children from underprivileged and neglected backgrounds are encouraged to explore their potential. It places a strong emphasis on values, discipline, and self-reliance. The staff and volunteers work closely with the children, creating a family-like environment filled with care, support, and emotional security. With decades of service and dedication, BVJSS has become a beacon of hope for the most vulnerable sections of society, helping shape a brighter and more inclusive future.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To provide a safe, nurturing, and inclusive home for orphaned, blind, deaf, and differently-abled children.

To foster values of empathy, discipline, and social responsibility among children.

To work towards breaking the cycle of marginalization and building pathways to meaningful and empowered lives.

Our Vision

To create an inclusive and compassionate society where every orphaned and differently-abled child is empowered through education, care, and opportunity to live a life of dignity, independence, and purpose.
